4  * Optimal Partition is a toolbox to solve special versions of the Set
5  * Partitioning Problem, that is the combinatorial optimisation of a
6  * decomposable objective over a set of feasible partitions (defined
7  * according to specific algebraic structures: e.g., hierachies, sets of
8  * intervals, graphs). The objectives are mainly based on information theory,
